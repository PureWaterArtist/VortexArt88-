## 🌪️ System Manifest & Symmetrical Fluid Propulsion Philosophy

The **Isothermal Implosion Propulsion Module (Project Aetheris-Core)** houses the geometric layouts, aerodynamic Venturi profiles, and fluid-dynamic calculation baselines for the four isolated wheel-hub flywheel assemblies. This module completely rejects heavy mechanical drivetrains, transmission shafts, differentials, and high-wear electric hub motors that introduce thermal resistance losses and experience severe material fatigue over a decade of continuous service.

Instead, the propulsion architecture relies entirely on **Symmetrical Implosion Geometry**. When the vehicle crosses its static initiation threshold, incoming atmospheric air mass is scooped into a front multi-axial array of six tapering Venturi Ram Intake Ports calibrated to a strict 5:1 volumetric compression ratio. This channels compressed, high-velocity air jets directly into internal **1.618 Golden Ratio Logarithmic Spiral Horns** wrapped within the wheel-hub frames. 

By forcing the fluid inward toward the center line of the track, the system creates **centripetal implosion**—the pressure increases at the core of the vortex while dropping at the track walls. The water compresses itself into a frictionless liquid rope that travels down the center of the channel lined with an atomic layer of hydrophobic, non-wetting **CVD Graphene**. This completely prevents the water from ever touching or sandblasting the track walls, providing a cavitation-free fluid drive running entirely on natural fluid mass momentum.
