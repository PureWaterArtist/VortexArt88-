This document provides the clear, optimized, step-by-step industrial workflows required to manufacture and assemble the vehicle chassis and propulsion core from raw, non-proprietary elements.

## 🖨️ Day 1: Multi-Axial SLA Monocoque Printing & Post-Curing
1.  **Prepare Resin Substrate Vats:** Fill the cleanroom industrial micro-SLA vats with a non-toxic composite liquid consisting of 45% ultra-fine Fused-Quartz Silicon powder and 55% Crystalline Nanocellulose (CNC) liquid tough resin suspension matrix.
2.  **Initiate Layer Slicing Run:** Load the 3D CAD data. Calibrate the UV laser to a 25-micron spot size. Print the main 5-passenger monocoque frame, internal logarithmic spiral lattice nodes, and 2.2mm logic tracks simultaneously. (Time: 14 Hours continuous run).
3.  **Evacuation Flush & Ultrasonic Bath:** Submerge the printed hull parts in a high-purity isopropyl alcohol wash. Engage ultrasonic agitations to completely clear out uncured resin from the internal Venturi intakes and fluidic channels. (Time: 4 Hours).
4.  **Photolytic Cross-Linking Pass:** Transfer components to a UV-thermal curing oven stabilized at 60°C to lock all structural dimensions and remove interior micro-stresses. (Time: 6 Hours).

## 🛰️ Day 2: CVD Graphene Deposition, Sputtering, & Solar Skin Application
1.  **Graphene Boundary Layer Coating:** Utilize Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) to deposit a single atomic layer of high-purity Graphene cleanly across the interior walls of the 2.2mm logic tracks and golden spiral channels to enforce a zero-drag hydrophobic super-slip floor. (Time: 8 Hours).
2.  **Node Metallization Pass:** Mask the non-conductive channels and mount the substrate blocks inside a Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) vacuum tank. Sputter high-purity gold to grow the low-voltage control tracks and internal actuation node pads to a depth of $1500\text{ \AA}$. (Time: 4 Hours).
3.  **Dielectric Spin-Coating:** Spin-coat an amorphous fluoropolymer Cytop resin layer evenly over the gold control paths. Vacuum-bake at 80°C to secure a seamless 1.2-micron insulating shield with a 15V dielectric breakdown barrier. (Time: 4 Hours).
4.  **Solar Film Deposition:** Spray-apply the organic, non-toxic Perovskite-Chlorophyll photovoltaic matrix directly onto the vehicle’s aerodynamic outer biopolymer skin hull. Heat-seal to verify a continuous $220\text{ W/m}^2$ radiant solar harvesting yield. (Time: 8 Hours).

## 🛠️ Day 3: Component Integration, Pressure Carpets, & Transducer Bonding
1.  **Piezoelectric Ribbon Inlay:** Position the 250-micron quartz-crystal piezoelectric ribbons inside the dead-end deceleration tracts. Secure the flexible PVDF ribbon carpets flush across the 5-passenger seating bases and the 450-liter rear cargo hold boards. (Time: 8 Hours).
2.  **Electro-Acoustic Array Mounting:** Install the 12 standalone 2,400 Hz acoustic transducers flanking the wheel fluid tracks. Interlink the nodes directly with the floor-mounted graphene ultracapacitor loop to manage the permanent standing harmonic anti-freeze ripple. (Time: 8 Hours).
3.  **Casing Interlock and Shell Integration:** Bolt and interlock the concentric 1.618 golden spiral wheel hub casings onto the monocoque bulkheads using zero-tool mechanical lock-tabs. Pot the wire interfaces inside a protective bio-rubber compound seal. (Time: 8 Hours).

2.  **Fluidic Bus Charging:** Vacuum-inject exactly 180.0 Liters of pure distilled water (45.0 Liters per wheel hub quadrant) into the track lines. Evacuate all air pockets to ensure solid hydraulic continuity. (Time: 4 Hours).
3.  **Isothermal Resonance Check:** Activate the 2,400 Hz acoustic array to verify low-drain winter shield performance.
