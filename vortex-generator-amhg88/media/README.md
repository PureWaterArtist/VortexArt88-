## 🎨 Digital Slicing & Rendering Guidelines

*   **Resolution Aspect Ratio:** Minimum $1920 \times 1080$ Full HD (Preferred $3840 \times 2160$ 4K Ultra-HD Master standard to capture micro-Tesla line depths).
