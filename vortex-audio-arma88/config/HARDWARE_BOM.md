# Project ARMA-88 Field Procurement Ledger & Materials Manual

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Biomimetic Acoustic Resonator Array (Project ARMA-88)** chassis and inner acoustic layers. ## 🪶 1. Ear-Canal Interface Chassis (Outer Shell)
