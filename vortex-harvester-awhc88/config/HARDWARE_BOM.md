# Project AWHC-88 Field Procurement Ledger & Materials Manual

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Atmospheric Water Harvester Condenser (Project AWHC-88)** core. All sourced components and engineering mediums must strictly meet or exceed these definitions to maintain absolute structural integrity under severe day-to-night desert temperature shifts.

---

## 🧱 1. Raw Polymer Mass Stock
*   **Primary Medium:** 1.5kg Spool of Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF) or High-Elasticity PETG filament.
*   **Alternate Medium:** High-Density Copper-Infused Filament (for high thermal conductivity printing zones).
*   **Function:** Forms the primary outer pressure housing casing, the multi-tier cardioid induction intake plenum, and the internal micro-Venturi compression extraction channels.

---

## 🌀 2. High-Efficiency Condenser Mesh Matrix
*   **Item Specification:** 99% Pure Oxygen-Free High-Conductivity (OFHC) Copper Mesh Screen (Wire diameter: 0.15mm–0.25mm).
*   **Volumetric Sizing:** Cut into custom geometric segments to fit the internal Φ-scale collection channels.
*   **Function:** Intercepts the high-velocity air streams at the precise point of localized static pressure drop, providing an instantaneous thermal heat-sink boundary where suspended vapor drops pass its dew-point threshold.

---

## 🧪 3. Food-Safe Hydrophobic Micro-Sealant
*   **Item Specification:** Clear, low-viscosity, food-safe, waterproof Polyurethane Sealant or specialized inert liquid ceramic liner.
*   **Quantity:** 100mL fluid volume required.
*   **Function:** Coats the 3D-printed internal extraction walls to completely eliminate micro-porosity weeping, ensuring harvested drinking water drains safely into the pool without contamination from raw plastics.

---

## 🔩 4. Interface Seals & Drainage Hardware
*   **Item A:** Food-Grade Silicone O-Rings (Sized to match the 15mm base drainage manifold port).
*   **Item B:** 15mm Outer Diameter Food-Safe Stainless Steel (inox 316L) or Borosilicate Glass Output Tube.
*   **Function:** Routes the fully condensed moisture volume safely out of the pressure housing into downstream filtration trays or sovereign collection reservoirs.
