## 🛠️ 4. The Low-Tech "Two-Bucket" Garage Prototype Blueprint

For builders, students, and off-grid engineers working with minimal budgets, this configuration establishes a fully functional testing engine using standard hardware store stock.

### A. Component Blueprint
*   **Chambers:** 2x 5-Gallon Clear Plastic Utility Pails (Polycarbonate/Polypropylene).
*   **Manifold:** 1.5-inch Schedule 40 PVC Pipe, 1x T-Fitting, 4x 90-Degree Elbows, 3x Slip Ball Valves.
*   **Drainage:** 2x 1.5-inch PVC Bulkhead Fittings threaded into the bottom center of each bucket.
*   **Propulsion:** 1x 1000-1200 GPH Submersible Pond Pump seated inside a lower 27-Gallon Heavy-Duty Storage Tote.

### B. Symmetrical Manifold Cut List (1.5-inch PVC)
To guarantee perfectly identical fluid friction and velocity inputs on both sides of the figure-8, pipes must be cut symmetrically:
*   **Pump Main:** 24 inches — Vertical lift from the pump to the master ball valve.
*   **Upper T-Feeder:** 12 inches — Connects the master valve to the bottom of your T-Junction.
*   **Symmetrical Wings:** 2x 18 inches — Extend horizontally out of the left and right sides of the T-Junction.
*   **Nozzle Drops:** 2x 8 inches — Drop vertically out of your branch ball valves to connect to the 90-degree elbows.

### C. Chamber Fabrication & Sealing
1.  **The Window:** Center a 4-inch wide by 10-inch tall rectangle on the flattest side of each bucket, starting 3.5 inches up from the bottom floor. Drill 1/2-inch pilot holes in the corners and cut cleanly using a fine-toothed jigsaw.
2.  **The Bolt Ring:** Clamp the buckets back-to-back. Mark bolt hole dots 1 inch away from the window edge every 2 inches around the perimeter. Drill through both buckets simultaneously using a **9/32-inch Step Drill Bit (Uni-bit)** to prevent plastic cracking.
3.  **The Seal:** Separate buckets, apply a thick 1/2-inch bead of **marine-grade 100% silicone** between the bolt ring and the window edge. Bolt the buckets back-to-back using 1/4-inch stainless steel bolts and washers. Hand-tighten evenly until the silicone squeezes out. Cure for 48 hours.
