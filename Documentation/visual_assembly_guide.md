2.  **Establish Water & Material Autonomy:** Deploy the printed logarithmic nozzles into local sand, clay, and waste polymer mixtures inside your **Geopolymer Foundry (Pillar 02)** to cast your dense obsidian geopolymer structural plates. 3.  **Initiate Self-Replication:** Assemble your **Universal Mother-Node (Pillar 23)** using your printed components, cast geopolymer frames, and raw copper solenoid wire wraps.
