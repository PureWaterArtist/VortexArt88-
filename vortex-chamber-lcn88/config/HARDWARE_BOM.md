# Project LCN-88 Field Procurement Ledger & Materials Manual

This document governs the physical procurement specification for the **Lemniscate Collision Node (Project LCN-88)** housing. All materials must meet or exceed these definitions to maintain absolute structural containment under high pump back-pressures.

## 🧱 1. Raw Polymer/Resin Mass Stock
*   **Primary Medium:** 1.0kg Spool of Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol (PETG) or Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF).
*   **Alternate Medium:** High-Impact/Tough Engineering UV Resin (for SLA cleanroom setups).
*   **Function:** Forms the outer protective boundary shell and inner logarithmic lemniscate fluid tracks.

## 🧪 2. Hydrophobic Core Boundary Sealant
*   **Item:** Clear, low-viscosity, food-safe Marine Grade Epoxy Resin.
*   **Quantity:** 50mL total volume required.
*   **Function:** Coats internal 3D layer lines to achieve a 100% watertight structural seal and prevent microscopic fluid weeping.

## 🔩 3. Flange Connectors & Interface Seams
*   **Item A:** 2x Equal-length strips of Heavy-Duty Marine Heat-Shrink Tubing (35mm Flat Width).
*   **Item B:** 2x High-Torque Stainless Steel Worm-Gear Hose Clamps (25mm-40mm Adjustment Band).
*   **Function:** Secures Nozzle A and Nozzle B into the side receiver sleeves under extreme centripetal vector shifts.

## 🪚 4. Sub-Manifold Plumbing Vectors
*   **Item A:** 2x Lengths of Clear Vinyl Tubing (Inner Diameter matched to your pump feed line).
*   **Item B:** 1x Segment of Flexible Silicone Aquarium Airline Tubing (Inner Diameter 4mm-6mm).
*   **Function:** Feeds the horizontal counter-rotating input lines and establishes the Z-axis atmospheric vacuum verification track.
