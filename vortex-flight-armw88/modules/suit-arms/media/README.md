## 📸 Workbench Telemetry & Visual Capture Protocols

To maintain absolute data integrity across public ledger submissions, all field images or videos uploaded to this matrix by the community must adhere to these baseline rules:

1.  **Holographic Sleeve Renders:** All CAD or OpenSCAD visualization exports must use a clean, neutral background color profile to keep internal guide grooves and shoulder pin alignment bores clearly visible.
2.  **Linear Track Validation Imagery:** High-resolution photographs demonstrating wing joint slider travel must show the assembly under maximum extension and include a standard dial gauge readout placed directly in the frame.
3.  **Triboelectric Static Wave Captures:** Static potential charge collections must be documented using calibrated digital electrometers or high-speed particle visualization layouts, verifying a minimum peak generation footprint of $450\text{V}$ under simulated wind shear.
