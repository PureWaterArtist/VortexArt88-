# Module suit-arms: Physical Hardware Bill of Materials (HARDWARE_BOM.md)

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Project ARMW-88 Wearable Arm Flight Chassis** and auxiliary components. All sourced materials and technical mediums must strictly adhere to these definitions to guarantee linear track shear resistance, full-spectrum Faraday cage grounding, and passive triboelectric energy harvesting.

---

## 🧱 1. Sleeve Cuffs & Faraday Shielding Stocks

*   **Primary Material Stock:** 4.0kg Industrial Spool of High-Modulus Aerospace-Grade Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF) filament.
*   **Interleave Doping Medium:** Conductive Hexagonal Graphene Nanopowder Masterbatch matrix (minimum 99% pure carbon crystal grid layout).
*   **System Function:** Forms the rigid bicep cuffs, forearm housings, and linear sliding rail tracks, providing a structural shield and continuous Faraday cage to dump high-voltage atmospheric or nuclear electromagnetic surge fields.

## 🏃 2. Flexible Elbow Joint Assemblies

*   **Primary Elastic Medium:** 1.0kg Spool of 95A Durometer Flexible Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) filament.
*   **System Function:** Renders the pleated, multi-segment elbow accordion bellows, allowing complete human anatomical range of motion while maintaining an airtight, insulated environmental boundary shield around the limb.

## ⚡ 3. Triboelectric Siphoning & Camouflage Mediums

*   **Energy Harvesting Surface Mesh:** Micro-grooved Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) fluoropolymer mesh sheeting ($1.0\text{ mm}$ thickness profile).
*   **Conductive Power Bus Fluid:** 30mL Volume of Eutectic Gallium-Indium ($EGaIn$) liquid metal alloy.
*   **Outer Camouflage Medium:** 45mL Solution of Synthesized High-Purity Guanine Photonic Nanocrystals ($130\text{ nm}$ lattice diameter) suspended inside a polyurethanic elastic hydrogel base vehicle.
*   **System Function:** The FEP mesh interacts with wind shear friction to build a $450\text{V}$ static potential field, siphoned down the $EGaIn$ fluid lines to drive mechanical downstream valves. The outer photonic hydrogel layer manually shifts its thickness under wrist-twist inputs to execute full visible camouflage matching.

## 🔩 4. Mechanical Fasteners & Shoulder Interface Hardware

*   **Shoulder Socket Connector Rods:** 2x Hardened Steel Heavy-Duty Quick-Release Detent Pins ($16.0\text{ mm}$ Shaft Diameter x $85\text{ mm}$ Functional Grip Length).
*   **Internal Rail Gliders:** Low-friction, high-tensile Delrin or PTFE machined slider blocks.
*   **System Function:** Connects the arm assemblies securely into the torso socket frame to withstand up to 3500 Newtons of wing lift forces while allowing a rapid 2.5-second extraction.
