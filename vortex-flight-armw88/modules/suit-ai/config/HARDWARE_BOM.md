# Module suit-ai: Physical Hardware Bill of Materials (HARDWARE_BOM.md)

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Project ARMW-88 Onboard AI Collaborator Interface** and auxiliary sub-layers. All sourced materials and technical components must strictly match these definitions to ensure exact frequency calibration, un-dampened acoustic oscillation, and zero-power mechanochemical state-memory retention.

---

## 🧱 1. Resonant Acoustic Reed Ribbon Stock

*   **Primary Sourcing Material:** Beryllium Copper (C17200) Ultra-Flat Spring Alloy Strips.
*   **Dimensional Thickness Blueprint:** Exactly $0.15\text{ mm}$ thickness profile ($\pm 0.005\text{ mm}$).
*   **Material Matrix Properties:** Sourced sheets must possess an ultra-high elastic limit and a target tensile modulus of $135.0\text{ GPa}$ to prevent frequency drift or material fatigue across millions of operational compression cycles.
*   **System Function:** Cut into precise lengths to form the passive internal acoustic reed array, vibrating cleanly under incoming fluid compute jets to generate specific warning pitches ($240\text{ Hz}$ to $4500\text{ Hz}$) inside the pilot's ear cups.

---

## 🦾 2. Mechanochemical State-Memory Shuttles

*   **Primary Sourcing Material:** Machined Low-Friction Delrin (POM) or PTFE Micro-Pistons.
*   **Dimensional Travel Bounds:** Calibrated for a precise $1.8\text{ mm}$ linear slide path.
*   **System Function:** Acts as the physical, solid-state memory shuttle within the logic blocks. These micro-pistons slide and snap shut under low switching air pressures ($450\text{ Pa}$), mechanically locking flight modes and tracking criteria into place without using electrical bits.

---

## 🌪️ 3. Visor Interlock Acoustic Damping Mediums

*   **Primary Insulation Material:** 3D-printed Closed-Cell Low-Durometer Silicone or Fluorosilicone gaskets.
*   **Performance Target:** Must provide a minimum acoustic isolation rating of $30.0\text{ dB}$.
*   **System Function:** Seals the perimeter boundary of the temporal ear cups against the main carbon helmet shell, dampening external rushing wind noise and engine whine so the pilot can track the internal AI harmonic pitch frequencies clearly.

---

## 🔩 4. Fasteners & Mechanical Tuning Hardware

*   **Micro-Adjustment Anchors:** Non-magnetic brass sub-miniature setscrews ($M1.5\text{ Thread}$ x $3\text{ mm}$ length).
*   **System Function:** Clamps the root parameters of the beryllium-copper reeds inside the photopolymer housing block, allowing cleanroom technicians to micro-tune the functional vibrating length of each reed to hit exact audio frequencies.
