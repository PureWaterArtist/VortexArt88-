# Module suit-torso: Cleanroom Post-Processing & Vortex Shield Quality Manual

This operational standard governs the verification protocols, post-processing steps, and defensive shield metrics for the **Project ARMW-88 Wearable Torso Flight Chassis**. These audits must be completed sequentially to certify structural shell containment and aerodynamic channel sealing before arm or wing sub-modules are integrated.

## 🔬 1. Post-Print Duct Inspection & Mechanical Lock Parity
*   **Vortex Slit Dimension Audit:** Scan the internal $3.5\text{ mm}$ jet pinch exit slits located along the collarbone and shoulder flanges using a high-magnification borescope. The tracks must be entirely free of structural stringing, over-extrusion burrs, or layer sagging. A discrepancy greater than $\pm 0.05\text{ mm}$ will destabilize the rotating kinetic air curtain, causing asymmetric boundary layer breakdown.
*   **Central Dial Interface Alignment:** Measure the internal registry path for the triple-lobe steel engagement plate using digital depth gauges. Verify alignment within $\pm 0.02\text{ mm}$. The interlocking steel bars must glide smoothly without scoring or binding within the side frame receptor paths.
*   **Thermal Settlement Phase:** Bake the completed load-bearing torso plates inside an industrial annealing chamber at a continuous $85^\circ\text{C}$ for a minimum of 6 hours to maximize inter-layer fusion and eliminate mechanical stress memory around the heavy wing anchor points.

## 🧲 2. Faraday Grid Grounding & Photonic Skin Integration
*   **Faraday Resistance Matrix Check:** Map electrical resistance (Ohms) by placing digital multimeter test probes across the extreme vertices of the front and back interlocking plates. The graphene-infused carbon-fiber polyethyl-carbonate matrix must show continuous electrical grounding paths under $12\text{ \Omega}$ across all surfaces. If any quadrant shows high electrical isolation, apply an interior layer of conductive nickel or pure graphite matrix compound.
*   **Chameleon Hydrogel Fluidic Injection:** Inject exactly $90\text{ mL}$ of the calibrated sub-micron guanine crystal elastomeric hydrogel matrix into the external $1.5\text{ mm}$ macro-groove skin tracks. Use vacuum-purging protocols to completely eliminate air bubble pockets within the channels.
*   **Pneumatic Breathing Loop Validation:** Apply a volumetric pressure cycle to the internal thoracic expansion tracks. Visually check the outer skin layer under a color spectrophotometer; the expansion must distribute evenly across the front rib panels, shifting the reflection band smoothly without cracking or peeling.

## 🩺 3. Resodynamic Vortex Shield & Structural Load Testing

*   **Vortex Shield Pressure Wave Check:** Mount the closed torso assembly onto a cleanroom aerodynamic test jig.
