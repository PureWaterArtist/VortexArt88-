# Module suit-legs: Physical Hardware Bill of Materials (HARDWARE_BOM.md)

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Project ARMW-88 Wearable Leg & Landing Gear Chassis** and auxiliary components. All sourced elements and raw stocks must meet these rigid definitions to guarantee landing gear force containment, full-spectrum Faraday cage grounding, and structural lower-limb impact survivability.

---

## 🧱 1. Upper Limb Armor & Faraday Shielding Stocks

*   **Primary Material Stock:** 6.0kg Industrial Spool of High-Modulus Aerospace-Grade Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF) filament.
*   **Interleave Doping Medium:** Conductive Hexagonal Graphene Nanopowder Masterbatch matrix (minimum 99% pure carbon crystal grid layout).
*   **System Function:** Forms the rigid thigh guards, shin sleeves, and lower chassis collars, acting as a high-strength structural shield and continuous Faraday cage to dump high-voltage atmospheric or nuclear electromagnetic surge fields.

## 🦘 2. Kangaroo-Tendon High-Elasticity Leaf Springs

*   **Primary Structural Spring Medium:** Multi-layered, high-elasticity pre-preg carbon fiber composite ribbon strands.
*   **Performance Calibration Target:** Rated for an operational spring rate up to $125,000\text{ N/m}$ and a maximum percussive energy absorption threshold of $860.0\text{ Joules}$ [unloaded state].
*   **System Function:** Forms the active lower shin and heel kinetic landing loop springs, flexing smoothly to absorb and redirect severe touchdown forces safely across the pilot's core torso frame, entirely bypassing skeletal spine loading.

## ⚡ 3. Lower Body Triboelectric Siphoning & Camouflage Mediums

*   **Energy Harvesting Surface Mesh:** Micro-grooved Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) fluoropolymer mesh sheeting ($1.0\text{ mm}$ thickness profile).
*   **Conductive Power Bus Fluid:** 30mL Volume of Eutectic Gallium-Indium ($EGaIn$) liquid metal alloy.
*   **Outer Camouflage Medium:** 65mL Solution of Synthesized High-Purity Guanine Photonic Nanocrystals ($130\text{ nm}$ lattice baseline pitch) suspended inside a polyurethanic elastic hydrogel base vehicle.
*   **System Function:** The FEP mesh siphons wind friction forces to generate a $450\text{V}$ static field potential across the $EGaIn$ lines to drive downstream mechanical joints. The outer photonic hydrogel layer manually compresses or relaxes under heel-twist inputs to execute full visible camouflage background matching.

## 🔩 4. Mechanical Fasteners & Hip Interface Hardware

*   **Primary Retaining Fasteners:** 2x Inox 316L Stainless Steel Marine-Grade Quick-Release Detent Pins.
*   **Pin Dimensional Target:** $14.0\text{ mm}$ Shaft Diameter x $65\text{ mm}$ Functional Grip Length, featuring spring-loaded double-ball locking mechanisms and integrated steel pull-rings.
*   **System Function:** Secures the leg assemblies directly into the base rim of the torso module, providing a heavy-duty connection capable of withstanding extreme multi-axial landing shocks while maintaining a rapid 3.0-second extraction constraint.
