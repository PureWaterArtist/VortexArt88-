## 🦾 1. Ingress & Pre-Flight Fluidic Calibration Protocol

2.  **Verify Gate Switching Parity:** Manually stroke the left chest test plunger twice. Listen for the characteristic acoustic tone from the exhaust ports, confirming that the physical fluidic flip-flop gates are switching cleanly between states.
3.  **Zero Altimetry Reference:** While standing flat at the launchpad baseline elevation, rotate the brass mechanical bias needle on the upper spine housing clockwise until the low-altitude static pressure chamber achieves exact equilibrium with local ambient air pressure.
4.  **Arm Ballistic Failsafe Loop:** Verify that the primary safety slider gate is clear of the pneumatic pilot valve. The emergency ballistic canopy circuit is now ARMED and responsive to the $1200\text{ Pa}$ critical stall threshold.
