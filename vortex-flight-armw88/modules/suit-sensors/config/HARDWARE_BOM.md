# Module suit-sensors: Physical Hardware Bill of Materials (HARDWARE_BOM.md)

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Project ARMW-88 Solid-State Fluidic Sensor Suite** and auxiliary components. All sourced materials and raw stocks must meet these rigid definitions to guarantee flawless sub-millimeter logic trace cross-linking, total EMP immunity, and multi-spectral passive threat mapping.

---

## 🧱 1. Fluidic Computer Core & Matrix Blocks

*   **Primary Sourcing Material:** 1.5kg High-Detail SLA UV Engineering Photopolymer Resin.
*   **Material Properties:** Must be a high-stiffness, zero-warp formulation (Rigid/Tough profiles) with a tensile modulus exceeding $2.8\text{ GPa}$ to prevent internal logic path flexing under peak pressure surges.
*   **System Function:** Forms the solid-state micro-channel matrix, carves out the internal $1.5\text{ mm}$ logic paths, and provides a continuous, warp-free platform for boundary-layer gas-dynamic calculations.

*   **Inlet Sizing Target:** $32.0\text{ mm}$ Opening Diameter tapering smoothly into the internal acoustic comparator circuit links.
*   **System Function:** Integrated directly onto the spine shell parameters to act as the raw sound capture funnel, turning the acoustic frequency signatures of incoming vectors into physical, compressed air jet inputs.

## 🏔 3. Pneumatic Altimetry Pitot Interconnects

*   **Primary Conduit Medium:** 4.5mm Bore Corrugated Fluorosilicone Tubing.
*   **Material Baseline Bounds:** High-flexibility, ultra-lightweight, and thermal-resistant across extreme high-altitude and low-altitude flight envelopes.
*   **System Function:** Establishes the continuous physical pressure lines running from the boot-mounted pitot tubes up into the torso's altimetry logic block to map terrain obstacles and ground-effect changes.

## 🔩 4. Fasteners & Mechanical Feedback Hardware

*   **Limb Interface Ports:** $2.0\text{ mm}$ Barb fittings and O-ring face gaskets machined from non-magnetic brass or high-tensile Delrin.
*   **Kinesthetic Cuff Bladders:** Microscopic flexible polyurethane balloon cells.
*   **System Function:** Feeds the tactile response pressure lines from the fluidic core output registers straight into the arm sleeve cuffs, enabling rapid, kinesthetic threat-alert inflation loops without electric signals.
