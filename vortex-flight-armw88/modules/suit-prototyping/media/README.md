## 📸 Workbench Telemetry & Visual Capture Protocols

To maintain absolute data integrity and strict audit verification across all public ledger submissions, all cleanroom staging images or video records uploaded here by the community must strictly adhere to these baseline rules:

1.  **Holographic Slicing Renders:** All printer slicing software simulation exports or printer queue snapshots must use a high-contrast dark or transparent background profile to keep infill configurations (50% Gyroid density targets) and perimeter tracks perfectly visible.
2.  **SLA Channel Post-Processing Documentation:** High-resolution photographs demonstrating successful internal micro-channel resin evacuation and filtered isopropyl alcohol flushes must leverage macro backlit illumination.
3.  **Drop-Tower and Calibration Telemetry:** High-speed capture records tracking structural compression tests on the dynamic testing sting balance must include a live timeline scale chart placed directly inside the frame to confirm compliance with the 14-day phase goals.
