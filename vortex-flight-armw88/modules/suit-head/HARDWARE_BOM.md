# Module suit-head: Physical Hardware Bill of Materials (HARDWARE_BOM.md)

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Project ARMW-88 Wearable Head Flight Chassis** and its removable biomimetic liner. All sourced materials and technical mediums must strictly adhere to these definitions to guarantee structural impact resistance, full-spectrum Faraday cage grounding, and passive thermal regulation.

---

## 🧱 1. Outer Shell & Faraday Shielding Stocks

*   **Primary Material Stock:** 2.0kg Industrial Spool of High-Modulus Aerospace-Grade Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF) filament.
*   **Interleave Doping Medium:** Conductive Hexagonal Graphene Nanopowder Masterbatch matrix (or high-purity conductive graphite spray treatment, minimum 99% pure carbon crystal content).
*   **Specification Bounds:** Filaments must be stored inside a desiccant drying bay at $<10\%$ relative humidity for a minimum of 12 hours before printing.
*   **System Function:** Forms the rigid outer aerodynamic head-shield clamshell mass, acting as a seamless, continuous, solid-state Faraday cage to completely ground out high-voltage electromagnetic surges (EMP).
