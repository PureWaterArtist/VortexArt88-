3.  **AI Interface Routing:** Connect the internal micro-fluidic conduit loops from the sensor block directly to the **AI Interface Array (`suit-ai`)**. Route the beryllium-copper acoustic reed tracks upward into the helmet-mating neckline collar.
4.  **Arm Sleeve Attachment:** Align the upper tongue joints of the left and right **Arm Modules (`suit-arms`)** with the torso shoulder sockets. Push the dual $16.0\text{ mm}$ hardened steel quick-release detent pins completely through the horizontal bores until they click closed.
5.  **Leg Exoskeleton Coupling:** Guide the upper load-bearing tongues of the left and right **Leg Modules (`suit-legs`)** into the bottom base rim receptors of the torso. Slide the dual $14.0\text{ mm}$ Inox 316L stainless steel detent pins through the transverse channels until locked.
6.  **Wing Spine Mounting:** Slide the root mounting box of the folding **Wing Structure Module (`suit-wings`)** flat against the backplate flange of the torso. Engage the secondary slide-rods and verify that the 4mm Bowden mechanical emergency jettison cable runs clear to the front chest override lever notch.
7.  **Forearm Linkage Lock:** Slide the wing skeleton's forearm gliders directly onto the external $14.0\text{ mm}$ linear sliding guide tracks of the arm sleeves. Verify that extending the forearms smoothly manipulates the wing fold linkages.
8.  **Helmet Seating:** Position the **Head Module (`suit-head`)** clamshell over the neck collar rim. Interlock the tongue-and-groove Faraday tracks with the torso collar bone boundaries and drive the dual 8mm jawline pins home.
