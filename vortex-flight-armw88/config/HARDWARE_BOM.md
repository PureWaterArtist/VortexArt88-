# Project ARMW-88 Field Procurement Ledger & Materials Manual

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Morphing Aero-Resonator Wing Matrix (Project ARMW-88)**. Because this is a life-critical, high-speed human flight envelope, all sourced elements and technical mediums must strictly meet or exceed these definitions to prevent structural failure under extreme dynamic loads.

---

## 🧱 1. Load-Bearing Exoskeleton & Spine Chassis Stock
*   **Primary Medium:** 8.0kg Industrial Spool of Aerospace-Grade Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF) or continuous carbon fiber matrix filament.
*   **Auxiliary Stock:** 12mm Solid Carbon Fiber Structural Support Rods.
*   **Function:** Forms the main rigid flight back-plate spine chassis, forearm slider tracking rails, and load-bearing limb sleeves that transfer aerodynamic lift safely across the pilot's core torso.

---

## 🦅 2. Aerodynamic Boundary Skins & Denticle Foil
*   **Primary Medium:** 4.0kg High-Compliance Flexible Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU, 95A Durometer scale).
*   **Function:** Forms the flexible wing web boundaries and the 3D-printed microscopic shark-denticle outer skins required to trap micro-vortices and maintain boundary layer attachment during high alpha pitch maneuvers.

---

## 🦘 3. Kangaroo-Tendon Deceleration Landing Gear (Modules Layer)
*   **Item Specification:** High-Elasticity Carbon-Fiber Leaf-Spring Loop Assemblies (Rated for an operational spring rate up to $125,000\text{ N/m}$).
*   **Hardware Interface:** Inox 316L Stainless Steel quick-release locking detent pins.
*   **Function:** Extends down the lower leg structures to form the passive kinetic impact landing gear, absorbing and displacing heavy vertical percussive ground forces during rough-field touchdowns.

---

## 🌪️ 4. Fluidic Sensor Suite & Ram-Jet Core
*   **Primary Medium:** High-Detail SLA UV Tough Engineering Photopolymer Resin.
*   **Function:** Forms the solid-state micro-channel fluidic computer block housed in the spine plate. It tracks dynamic ram-pressures across $1.5\text{ mm}$ tracks to run air-velocity data tracking without electric micro-chips.

---

## 🪂 5. Emergency Safety Canopy Subsystem (Modules Layer)
*   **Item Matrix:** Ultra-Lightweight, High-Tensile Ripstop Kevlar or Military-Grade Nomex Matrix Material.
*   **Activation Unit:** Parametric 3D-printed pneumatic high-pressure release valve.
*   **Function:** Feeds into the ballistic emergency backup canopy module, automatically deploying the safety drag matrix via passive air pressure shifts if low-altitude stall threshold limits are crossed.
