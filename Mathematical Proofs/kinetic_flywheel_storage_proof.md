## 1. Topography & Inertial Mechanics of the Vitrified Toroidal Ring

$$\mathcal{I} = M \cdot R^2 \quad \left[\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^2\right]$$

$$\omega = \frac{RPM \cdot 2\pi}{60} \quad \left[\text{rad}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}\right]$$

The total structural Potential Kinetic Energy ($\mathbf{E}_{\text{kinetic}}$) stored safely inside the spinning mass is proved via the foundational mechanical equation:

$$\mathbf{E}_{\text{kinetic}} = \frac{1}{2}\mathcal{I} \cdot \omega^2 = \frac{1}{2}\left(M \cdot R^2\right) \cdot \omega^2 \quad \left[\text{Joules}\right]$$

$$\sigma_{\text{hoop}} = \rho_g \cdot v_p^2 = \rho_g \cdot \left(\omega \cdot R\right)^2 \quad \left[\text{Pascals}\right]$$

$$\sigma_{\text{hoop}} < \mathbf{T}_{\text{structural\_limit}} \quad (\approx 120\,\text{MPa})$$
