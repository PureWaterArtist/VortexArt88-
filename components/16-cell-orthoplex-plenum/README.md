# 16-Cell Orthoplex Flow Plenum

The **16-Cell Orthoplex Flow Plenum** is a high-capacity multi-channel distribution, partition, and manifold intake component. Grounded in a 4-dimensional cross-polytope (hyper-octahedron) geometric framework, this module operates as a zero-bottleneck high-volume distribution engine. It safely manages extreme volumetric capacities up to 200 Liters Per Minute (LPM) by balancing flow vectors along four mutually orthogonal spatial axes before smoothly dividing the intake mass into a symmetric 16-way tetrahedral discharge array.

## 📐 4D Cross-Polytope Geometry & Hyper-Orthogonal Plenums

Standard fluid manifolds experience massive internal shockwaves, cross-channel back-pressure rebounds, and severe boundary-layer skin friction when scaling up to high flow capacities. This plenum completely bypasses those classical hydro-mechanical bottlenecks by expanding fluid layers across a 16-celled hyper-octahedral projection:

* **Hyper-Orthogonal Load Balancing:** While the physical 3D housing handles real-space Cartesian coordinates (X, Y, Z), the internal configuration introduces an independent 4th spatial direction (W-axis) scaled by the Golden Ratio (Φ ≈ 1.618).
* **Frictionless Volumetric Scaling:** By mathematically elongating the velocity profiles along this virtual hyper-volume axis, mass expansion is distributed proportionally to vertical progression, letting the chamber absorb extreme fluid spikes without cross-channel drag.
* **Symmetrical 16-Cell Tetrahedral Split:** The plenum branches the incoming 64mm volume into a balanced 16-way spatial matrix (representing the 16 bounding tetrahedra of a 4D cross-polytope), ensuring flawless fluid handoffs to downstream channels.
