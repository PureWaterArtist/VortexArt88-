# Hyperboloidal Flow Regulator

The **Hyperboloidal Flow Regulator** is an advanced fluid-dynamics and kinetic-stabilization component. Grounded in a 3D doubly ruled quadric surface framework, this module maps a continuous hyperboloid of one sheet profile generated by rotating a hyperbola around its minor axis. It functions as an uninhibited transition engine—guiding incoming velocity profiles along straight horizontal coordinate tracks that slant through a narrow central throat before expanding out symmetrically into a wide discharge zone, completely eliminating cross-channel shear friction, boundary-layer drag, and pressure bottlenecks with zero moving parts.

## 📐 Doubly Ruled Geometry & Asymptotic Slant Symmetry

Traditional fluid junctions channel mass along rigid, bounded linear trajectories or crash fluid feeds abruptly into linear intersections, triggering massive internal back-pressure spikes, heavy skin friction, and kinetic stagnation. This regulator completely bypasses those mechanical restrictions by yielding completely to an infinite, continuous doubly ruled surface:

* **Doubly Ruled Quadric Geometry:** The internal housing tracks a mathematical hyperboloid where two independent sets of completely straight lines cross each other at every single point, eliminating boundary-layer separation waves.
* **Continuous Asymptotic Transition:** Fluid volume moves smoothly through an optimized 25mm throat radius (a), flared out proportionally to an exact 40mm asymptotic vertical scale parameter (c).
* **Frictionless Velocity Transitions:** By aligning the housing contours to a natural quadric balance curve, velocity fields naturally expand and rotate under pure shape guidance, completely grounding out cross-channel shear and boundary-layer drag to absolute zero.

## 🛠 Manufacturing & Hardening Standards

Because this component forces fluid streams to continuously loop and weave through intense spatial crossovers under massive centripetal loads, the internal channel walls experience complex fatigue. It must be fabricated using maximum structural print constraints:
* **Recommended Material:** Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate, PEEK, or technical composite tough resin.
* **Perimeter Wall Loops:** 10 (Mandatory to maximize lateral bond strength and prevent micro-fracturing along layer lines).
* **Infill Profile:** 100% Solid Infusion utilizing a Gyroid internal path arrangement.
