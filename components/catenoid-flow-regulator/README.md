# Catenoid Flow Regulator

The **Catenoid Flow Regulator** is an advanced fluid-dynamics and kinetic-stabilization component. Grounded in a 3D minimal surface framework, this module maps a continuous catenoid profile generated by rotating a catenary curve around an offset axis. It functions as a complete friction-neutralizing transition engine—guiding incoming velocity profiles along hyperbolic boundaries to allow fluid layers to move smoothly from a tightly compressed throat out into a wide, flanged expansion flare with zero mean curvature, completely eliminating boundary-layer drag, skin friction, and exit cavitation spikes.

## 📐 Minimal Surface Curvature & Hyperbolic Dynamics

Traditional fluid manifolds channel mass along rigid, bounded linear trajectories or squeeze volume through harsh angular reducers, forcing fluid layers to slam violently against internal chamber walls. This creates intense skin friction, micro-turbulent back-pressure spikes, and downstream fluid stagnation. This regulator completely bypasses those structural bottlenecks by utilizing a boundaryless minimal surface:

* **Zero Mean Curvature Geometry:** The internal profile matches a mathematical catenoid where outward tension and inward compression balance exactly at every single coordinate point (H=0).
* **Hyperbolic Expansion Flare:** Fluid volume moves smoothly through an optimized throat parameter constant (c=25mm), flared out proportionally to an exact 54.308mm discharge radius.
* **Frictionless Velocity Transitions:** By aligning the housing contours to a natural catenary balance curve, velocity fields naturally expand and decelerate under pure shape guidance, completely grounding out exit cavitation spikes and skin friction.

## 🛠 Manufacturing & Hardening Standards

Because this component subjects fluid streams to intense velocity expansion and structural deceleration along a continuous curved throat, the inner shell experiences high hydrodynamic load shifts. It must be fabricated using maximum structural print constraints:
* **Recommended Material:** Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate, PEEK, or technical composite tough resin.
* **Perimeter Wall Loops:** 10 (Mandatory to maximize lateral bond strength and prevent micro-fracturing along layer lines).
* **Infill Profile:** 100% Solid Infusion utilizing a Gyroid internal path arrangement.
