* **Polychoric Load Balancing:** While the physical 3D housing handles real-space Cartesian coordinates (X, Y, Z), the internal configuration introduces an independent 4th spatial direction (W-axis) scaled by the Golden Ratio (Φ ≈ 1.618).
* **Frictionless Volumetric Scaling:** By mathematically elongating the velocity profiles along this virtual hyper-volume axis, mass expansion is distributed proportionally to vertical progression, letting the chamber absorb extreme fluid spikes without cross-channel drag.
* **Symmetrical 24-Cell Octahedral Split:** The plenum branches the incoming 64mm volume into a balanced 24-way spatial matrix (representing the 24 bounding octahedra of a 4D regular 24-cell), ensuring flawless fluid handoffs to downstream channels.
