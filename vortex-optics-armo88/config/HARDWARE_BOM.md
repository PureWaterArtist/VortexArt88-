# Project ARMO-88 Field Procurement Ledger & Materials Manual

This document governs the physical procurement specifications for the **Biomimicry Optoelectronic Resonator Array (Project ARMO-88)** chassis and optical layers. All sourced mediums must strictly meet or exceed these definitions to preserve sub-micron alignment and sustain passive quantum upconversion kinetics.

---

## 🧱 1. Frame Structural Lattice Stock
*   **Primary Medium:** 1.0kg Spool of Carbon Fiber Polycarbonate (PC-CF) or Continuous Carbon Fiber Composite filament.
*   **Function:** Forms the ultra-rigid outer structural frame, temple arms, and mounting chassis plates to protect the sub-wavelength optics stack from thermal and torsional displacement.

---

## 💎 2. Flat-Optics Metalens Element Substrate
*   **Primary Medium:** Optical Grade Silicon Nitride ($Si_3N_4$) wafer plates, or Fused Silica Quartz blanks.
*   **Alternate Medium:** High-Transmissivity Ultra-Clear Tough Photopolymer UV Resin (for precision SLA lithography setups).
*   **Function:** Serves as the raw flat substrate for the sub-wavelength dielectric nano-pillar matrix to modulate photon phase fields without bulky glass refraction.

---

## 🧪 3. Quantum-Well Phosphor Doping Agents
*   **Item Specification:** High-Purity Copper-Doped Zinc Sulfide ($ZnS:Cu$) Crystal Nanopowder (Average particle size: $<50\text{ nm}$).
*   **Function:** Layered behind the primary metalens matrix to form the passive quantum dotwells that execute un-driven upconversion luminescence, converting ambient environmental infrared and cosmic starlight noise directly into visible green light ($555\text{ nm}$).

---

## 🌀 4. Adaptive Fluidic Focus Medium
*   **Item Specification:** Eutectic Gallium-Indium ($EGaIn$) Liquid Metal Alloy, or high-refractive-index, non-toxic optical-grade mineral oil.
*   **Quantity:** 10mL fluidic volume required.
*   **Function:** Fills the micro-fluidic, dual-concentric capillary rings surrounding the lenses, using Coandă-effect pneumatic pressure shifts to dynamically distort the lens boundaries for variable mechanical zooming.

---

## 🔩 5. Sealing & Integration Hardware
*   **Item A:** Medical-Grade Fluorosilicone Micro-O-Rings (chemical-resistant to liquid metal alloys).
*   **Item B:** Low-Viscosity optical UV-cure adhesive (Index-matched to the photopolymer resin substrate).
*   **Function:** Hardens the perimeter boundaries of the multi-layered optical sandwich, ensuring a 100% hermetic vacuum seal that prevents internal dust or atmospheric moisture contamination.
