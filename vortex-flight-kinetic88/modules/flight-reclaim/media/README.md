## 📸 Technical Asset Verification & Cleanroom Camera Protocols

To maintain absolute data integrity and verifiable prior-art across all open-source submissions to the public ledger, all laser-interferometer snapshots, material line traces, or electrical feedback logs uploaded here by the community must strictly adhere to these formatting rules:

1.  **High-Contrast Inversion Renders:** All CAD or fluid-dynamic simulation exports must use a clean, dark-mode background profile to ensure that the reverse hydraulic paths and pressure node limits are perfectly visible on workshop monitors.
2.  **Quartz Element Documentation:** High-resolution macro photography demonstrating successful deposition of the 250-micron quartz piezoelectric ribbons and their surface gold traces must be shot under backlit cross-lighting.
3.  **Feedback Power Logs:** Visual data overlays tracking the 18.5% minimum energy recovery floor and voltage generation peaks must link directly back to the parameters calculated inside the root `master-flight-twin.py` digital twin script.
