These clumsy systems experience high defect rates and continuous material failure due to extreme stress points.

The micro-implosion of each bubble focuses energy into a sub-atomic plasma micro-jet that spikes locally to an explosive **5.5 GPa** of crushing pressure and **2000°C** of localized thermal spark. 

This extreme energy envelope cuts across the molecular bands, forcing the carbon atoms to instantly snap from loose sp² graphitic orientations into a flawless, zero-defect sp³ diamond cubic crystal lattice on the centrally positioned seed collection plate.
