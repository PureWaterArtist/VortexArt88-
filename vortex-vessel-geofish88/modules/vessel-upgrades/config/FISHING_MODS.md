# Module vessel-upgrades: Authorized Open-Source Rigging Patches & Utility Mods

This ledger details the structural constraints and mechanical specifications for the four primary community modifications engineered to maximize angling utility without warping the folding hull.

---

## 🎣 1. Symmetrical Stabilizing Hardshell Outriggers

*   **Mechanical Modification:** Fabricators clamp a dual-segmented, folding $4.0\text{ mm}$ copolymer polypropylene wing spar framework directly across the midship T-slot rails.
*   **The Physics:** The spars extend outward by $750.0\text{ mm}$ on each flank, terminating in small, secondary Miura-folded hardshell pontoons. This expansion increases the total waterline plane area during active casting.
*   **The Result:** The metacentric height ($GM$) increases from $+0.42\text{ m}$ to a rock-solid **$+0.88\text{ m}$**, allowing the angler to confidently stand up and cast fly lines or lean heavily over the gunwale to net trophy fish with zero capsizing risks.

---

## ⚓ 2. Zero-Piercing Transom Anchor Drop Assembly

*   **Mechanical Modification:** A lightweight, over-center clamping bracket made from anodized 6061-T6 aluminum snaps directly over the rear stern transom panel lip.
*   **The Physics:** The bracket routes a internal braided nylon rope line through a mini polymer pulley wheel, dropping a $3.5\text{ kg}$ river anchor directly off the centerline of the vessel.
*   **The Result:** By routing the line precisely down the center keel axis, the anchor force cannot apply localized twisting or asymmetric torque loads to the hull's live-hinges, preserving joint tracking alignment under swift river currents.

---

## 🔋 3. Rail-Mounted Solid-State Solar Charge Bench

*   **Mechanical Modification:** Technicians thin-film laminate a flexible, marine-grade $60\text{ Watt}$ solar panel array directly across the top horizontal surface of the molded $HDPE$ structural bulkhead seat.
*   **The Physics:** Weatherproof wiring routes flush inside the side panel valley channels, terminating in a sealed polymer dry-box container positioned beneath the seat tracks.
*   **The Result:** The array charges a small $12\text{V}$ lithium iron phosphate ($LiFePO_4$) battery, providing un-jammable, zero-emission electrical juice to power portable fish-finders and navigation LED light sticks for night fishing without cluttering the cockpit floor.

---

## 📦 4. Modular Slide-Lock Bait Locker & Rod Holder Towers

*   **Mechanical Modification:** A high-capacity bait storage crate engineered from laser-cut polypropylene sheet segments quick-snaps onto the rear hull floorboards.
*   **The Physics:** The locker base uses sliding detent keys that slot directly into the vertical locking tracks behind the pilot. Vertical tubes on the flanks serve as structural rocket-launcher rod holders.
*   **The Result:** The gear cargo load is kept low along the absolute deck floorboard plane. This position maintains an incredibly low center of gravity ($G$), maximizing secondary rolling stability while keeping trolling rods angled precisely at $45.0^\circ$ for clean line layouts.
