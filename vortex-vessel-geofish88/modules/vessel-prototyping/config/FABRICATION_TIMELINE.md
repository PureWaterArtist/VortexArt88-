# Module vessel-prototyping: 4-Day Rapid Fabrication Scheduling Ledger

This document details the exact 4-day production steps, machine setups, and assembly test gates required to materialize the physical hardshell prototype boat from raw sheet stocks.

---

## 📅 Day 1: Sheet Nesting, CNC Laser Setup & Panel Routing (Phase 1)

### A. Raw Polypropylene Sheet Preparation
*   **Execution Matrix:** Secure the $2850\text{ mm} \times 1250\text{ mm}$ continuous $4.0\text{ mm}$ thick copolymer polypropylene raw stock flat onto a 3-axis CNC router vacuum bed table. Inspect the sheet faces to verify a minimum 3% active UV-stabilizer compound saturation.
*   **Timeline Target:** Manual sheet staging, zeroing Cartesian coordinates ($0, 0$), and executing vacuum clamping loops requires **4 hours**.

### B. V-Groove Crease Carving & Scoring
*   **Execution Matrix:** Load a high-speed $90.0^\circ$ carbide V-groove carving bit into the tool holder. Set spindle speed to $14,000\text{ RPM}$ and feed rate to $3200\text{ mm/min}$. Execute tool paths to score all mountain and valley fold lines.
*   **Timeline Target:** Continuous machine routing time requires **4 hours**. The bit must score down to a precise depth of $2.2\text{ mm}$, leaving a remaining live-hinge material web thickness of exactly $1.8\text{ mm} \pm 0.05\text{ mm}$.

---

## 📅 Day 2: Post-Machining Edge Dressing & Latch Installation (Phase 2)

### A. Live-Hinge Flex Treatment & Joint Dressing
*   **Execution Matrix:** Run a micro-deburring blade along all routed channels to clear plastic fuzz strings. Manually break and pre-flex every mountain and valley line through a full $180\text{-degree}$ travel path to align the polyolefin crystalline matrices.
*   **Timeline Target:** Full manual joint dressing and pre-flex tracking requires **4 hours**.

### B. Over-Center Latch Riveting
*   **Execution Matrix:** Align the marine-grade 316 stainless steel adjustable draw latch brackets against the pre-drilled layout marks. Drive $M4.0$ stainless steel pan-head pop rivets to clamp brackets onto the panel walls.
*   **Timeline Target:** Securing all 8 latch plates and cross-tightening fasteners to an ultimate torque margin of $3.8\text{ N-m}$ requires **4 hours**.

---

## 📅 Day 3: Sealing Bead Fusion & Bulkhead Assembly (Phase 3)

### A. Co-Extruded EPDM Sealing Integration
*   **Execution Matrix:** Clean all mating panel lips with a grease-releasing cleaner agent. Lay down the continuous $45\text{-Durometer}$ hollow D-profile EPDM bulb seals into the perimeter tracking channels. 
*   **Timeline Target:** Pressing seals into place and hot-welding joint splices via a mini thermal gun tool requires **4 hours**.

### B. Internal Molded Seat Track Welding
*   **Execution Matrix:** Symmetrically align the glass-reinforced vertical floor track rails onto the hull floor sheet sections. Use a plastic extrusion welding gun to fuse the rail bases permanently to the $HDPE$ deck plate.
*   **Timeline Target:** Rail deposition, hot weld cooling stabilization, and checking detent spring tabs requires **4 hours**.

---

## 📅 Day 4: Hydrostatic Vacuum Decay Check & Tank Testing (Phase 4)

### A. Joint Seal Vacuum Audit (Gate 1 Check)
*   **Execution Matrix:** Attach regional suction cups across the joint seams. Apply an analog draw pressure of $-20.0\text{ kPa}$. The maximum pressure decay loss must not exceed $0.05\text{ kPa}$ over a continuous 5-minute hold window. Requires **4 hours**.

### B. Water Displacement & Tank Stabilization Validation (Gate 2 Check)
*   **Execution Matrix:** Transport the fully expanded hardshell to a test tank bay. Load a calibrated $115.0\text{ kg}$ payload weight mass squarely onto the bulkhead seat. Verify that water displacement profiles hit a shallow draft of $5.58\text{ cm}$, leaving $25.55\text{ cm}$ of safe gunwale freeboard.
*   **Timeline Target:** Dynamic roll testing, metacentric balance checking ($GM \geq +0.42\text{ m}$), and final platform teardown requires **4 hours**. Prototype cleared for deployment.
